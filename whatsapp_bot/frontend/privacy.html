Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our job matching service:

  • Job preferences: Search criteria, job types, salary expectations, and location preferences you provide
  • Contact information: Your WhatsApp phone number for communication
  • Usage data: Basic analytics to improve our service (anonymized)
  • Conversation data: Messages you send to Aremu for job matching purposes

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Job matching: Find and send you relevant job opportunities based on your criteria
  • Communication: Send job alerts and respond to your queries via WhatsApp
  • Service improvement: Enhance our AI matching algorithms and user experience
  • Customer support: Provide assistance when you need help with our service

Data Protection & Security

We take your privacy seriously and implement robust security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • No selling: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • Secure storage: Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Regular audits: We conduct regular security assessments and updates
  • Compliance: We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other international data protection standards

WhatsApp Integration

Our service operates through WhatsApp with these privacy protections:

  • Opt-in only: We only send messages you've explicitly requested
  • Easy opt-out: Stop receiving messages anytime by saying "stop" or "unsubscribe"
  • Limited access: We don't access your other WhatsApp conversations or contacts
  • No storage: We don't store your WhatsApp messages beyond what's needed for job matching
  • WhatsApp's privacy: Your WhatsApp data remains subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your data at any time (see detailed instructions)
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Withdraw consent: Stop using our service at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Active users: Data is kept while you use our service
  • Inactive users: Data is automatically deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Deleted accounts: All data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Third-Party Services

We use these trusted third-party services:

  • WhatsApp Business API: For messaging (subject to Meta's privacy policy)
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered job matching (data is not stored by OpenAI)
  • Cloud hosting: Secure servers for data storage and processing
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics (no personal data shared)

We ensure all third parties meet our privacy and security standards.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in different countries:

  • Primary storage: Secure data centers with appropriate safeguards
  • Legal basis: Adequate protection mechanisms in place
  • Your rights: Same privacy rights apply regardless of location

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll notify you via WhatsApp before changes take effect
  • The "Last updated" date will be revised
  • Continued use of our service means you accept the updated policy
  • You can always request deletion if you disagree with changes

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights:

  • WhatsApp: Message us directly through our service
  • Email: privacy@aremu.ai
  • Data Deletion: Follow our detailed deletion instructions
  • Response time: We'll respond within 48 hours

Quick deletion: Simply message "delete my data" via WhatsApp for immediate processing.
